Rules

The rules for the room are simple:

  • Before asking a question here, ask it on Stack Overflow or another Stack Exchange site.
  • Do not fire off a question before you've researched your problem. Remember, Google is always your friend!
  • Use English to the best of your ability and do not use chatspeak. Just try to make sense.
  • Please don't bother ping spamming. This is the practice of constantly posting @username in order to annoy the person.
  • Try to limit inchat code to a reasonable amount. If you have lots of code to share, how about making a gist , or even a repository . You can also use pastebin, pastie and hastebin. If you post code, it will be removed from chat.
  • Don't flag stuff unless it's obviously flagworthy. It just annoys whoever invalidates the flags and doesn't help anyone.
  • Do not be a help vampire. This is, attempting to find help and lure attention onto your problems continuously where others show no clear interest in your problems.
  • Don't be creepy, no racism, hate or severe swearwords! Let's be friendly!
  • Remember this room has no one topic and people are free to talk about what they wish as long as it follows above rules and is loosely related to programming.
  • Multi-line messages shall be treated as a crime in this chatroom.
These are all nonstrict and really we'll get along fine if you're not begging for code and help all the time. In other words, be excellent to each other.
